PREPOSITIONAL CASE EXAMPLES

The prepositional case in Russian is used for objects of prepositions.

In English, we would say "the beaver lives in Oregon", but

in Russian, we would actually change the spelling of the Russian word for Oregon if it was used in this manner


Орегон - это штат. Бобер живет в Орегоне. (Oregon is a state. The beaver lives in Oregon.)

(note that Орегон changes spelling when it is used as в Орегоне in a prepositional phrase)
