INSTRUMENTAL CASE EXAMPLES

The instrumental case in Russian is used when saying "with" or "by means of".

For example, if we tried to say "HTML with CSS", we would use instrumental case.

A double example would be in the sentence "She and I eat caviar with sour cream."

Here, both "she" and "sour cream" are converted to instrumental case: мы с ней едим икру со сметаной.

Several nuances here: we use ней as the instrumental case for "she", we use the first person plural conjugation of the verb "to eat"
and we use the мы ("we") in lieu of "I" as part of the subject.
